Shackles of the Dandelion Gladiator

Weapon Ascension Material. Vennessa was not truly bound by her chains. Dream of the Dandelion Gladiator

Weapon Ascension Material. Vennessa may have been freed from slavery, but she then found herself subject to many other, even greater forms of bondage. What she really hoped for, in truth, was that the world might grow stronger.
